About Us

Built to bridge the gap between landlords and reliable maintenance

Established to bridge the gap between commercial landlords and reliable maintenance coverage, we operate as the in-house operations team for property portfolios across the North West.

Our coordinator, Sarah Connolly, manages contractor compliance, scheduling, and client liaison. We focus on keeping properties compliant, tenancies uninterrupted, and maintenance costs predictable.

Whether you manage a single commercial block or a mixed portfolio, we provide the operational backbone to keep your buildings running.
